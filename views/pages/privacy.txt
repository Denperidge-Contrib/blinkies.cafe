blinkies.cafe does not collect personally identifiable data.

to maintain the basic functionality of this site, we log blinkie inputs:
  - blinkie text
  - blinkie style
  - blinkie scale

you can opt out of logging: if you add "\nolog " (without quotes) at the start of your custom text, your blinkie inputs from not be logged.

we do *not* collect any data that identifies individual users in any way.
