about blinkie gifs

blinkies are tiny, 150x20-pixel animated gifs used as badges to share interests and tell jokes! They were popular decorations in early-2000s Geocities-era personal websites.

blinkies.cafe is a tool to generate custom blinkies, choosing from a variety of styles and adding whatever text you like!

blinkies are great for:

  • badges for your website or blog, to show off your interests!
  • eye-catching links on your streaming page!
  • sparkly messages in chat apps!

Also check out the blinkies.cafe chrome extension !!
